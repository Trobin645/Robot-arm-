3D PRINTING SETTINGS
This model is optimized for FDM 3D printing. The following print settings should be used: 0.2 mm layer height, 20-30% infill, supports are needed. The servos and mechanical connections used require robust and precise print quality, especially at the joints.
Recommended 3D Printing Settings:
Layer Height: 0.2 mm - For a good balance between detail and print speed.
Infill Density: 20-30% - Provides good structural integrity while minimizing material use.
Infill Pattern: Cubic - A strong pattern suitable for mechanically loaded parts.
Wall Thickness: 1.2 mm to 1.3 mm - To ensure that the walls of the parts are strong enough.
Supports: Enabled - Important to support overhangs on some of the more complex parts.
